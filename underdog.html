About Underdog

Underdog is a lovable canine superhero who first appeared on television in 1964. Created by W. Watts Biggers, he's known for his humble demeanor, his distinctive rhyming speech pattern, and his incredible superpowers that emerge when he takes his special "super energy pill."


Despite being called "Underdog," he's anything but! This cape-wearing beagle protects Capital City from various villains and disasters with his incredible strength, speed, and flight abilities.
